Stat Glossary

BA — Batting Average. Hits divided by at-bats.

HR — Home Runs. Total home runs hit.

OPS — On-base Plus Slugging. Measures a hitter's ability to get on base and hit for power.

WAR — Wins Above Replacement. How many wins a player adds compared to a replacement level player.

ERA — Earned Run Average. Average earned runs allowed per 9 innings.

IP — Innings Pitched. Total innings a pitcher has thrown.

SO — Strikeouts. Total batters struck out.

WAR — Wins Above Replacement. How many wins a player adds compared to a replacement level player.

How to Use

Hitters / Pitchers — Switch between the hitting and pitching roster.

Career Stats — Toggle between the 2026 season stats and each player's career stats.

Sort — Click any column header to sort players by that stat. Click again to reverse the order.
